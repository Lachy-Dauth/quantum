## Block Math Test

A general qubit state is written as:

$$
\ket{\psi} = \alpha\ket{0} + \beta\ket{1}
$$

where $|\alpha|^2 + |\beta|^2 = 1$.

The inner product of two states:

$$
\braket{\phi}{\psi} = \alpha^*\gamma + \beta^*\delta
$$
